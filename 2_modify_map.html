Hiking Trails Near the Univeristy of Minnesota Duluth

By: Ben Tyberg

Nothern Minnesota is known as a great place to get outdoors. While some excellent state parks lay both north of Duluth along the shore and south down the St. Louis River, there is no need to travel outside the city to find fabulous hiking options. Aimed towards students who may not be able to travel far, this map shows a few trails within walking distance of the Univeristy of Minnesota Duluth campus .
